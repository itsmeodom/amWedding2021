The Story

Melissa Tran & Adam Araza

  • December 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In true millennial fashion, Melissa and Adam met on a dating app called Coffee Meets Bagel. Adam gave the impression he knew how to scuba dive (he didn’t) and Melissa slyly indicated she did not live with her parents (she did).

  • February 2, 2016

    A Relationship is Born

    Before a spontaneous trip to San Diego, their relationship became official! Adam officially asked Melissa out at a mediocre boba shop. Luckily, the mediocrity of the boba was not a sign of things to come.

  • April 15, 2017

    It's Always Sunnier

    After realizing they really liked each other, Melissa finally convinced Adam that it made more sense to live closer to work (and her parents) than to live closer to Downtown San Jose. Melissa, Adam, and Waffles started their new life together in Sunnyvale, CA; the pain of living in suburbia was off-set by living a short drive away from an In-n-Out.

  • January 27, 2020

    The Proposal

    Adam proposed to Melissa in the living room of their Cupertino duplex. With encouragement from Waffles, Adam got down on his knee, (just two months after knee surgery!) and popped the question. Melissa said “Yes”, then bought a coffee table to make sure no one else can have a proposal in the same space.

Accomodations

We will be booking blocks of rooms at two hotels in San Diego. Use the links below or call and mention our wedding and receive a discounted rate. Please reach out to Melissa or Adam if the hotel says there are no more rooms available.


Springhill Suites San Diego Downtown Bayfront

At 900 Bayfront Court, this hotel is on the same property as our venue. It is also a short walk from the Gaslamp Quarter and Little Italy. Rooms from $159. Does not include parking. Pet friendly for an additional fee.




TownePlace Suites San Diego Airport/Liberty Station

At 2311 Lee Court, this hotel is only a short ride away from the airport and our venue. The room rate includes parking. Rooms from $179. Includes parking. Pet friendly for an additional fee.

Coronavirus

We are taking as many precautions as we can while we plan our wedding. In the event that it is deemed unsafe to hold a large gathering in San Diego County, we will take any necessary measures to ensure we get married before 2022.


Vaccinations

For the safety of our families and friends, we ask that all guests be fully vaccinated for Covid-19 before attending our wedding. If you are unable to receive the vaccination, we politely ask that you decline our invite.


Can I Bring my Kids?

Our event is restricted to adults ages 18+. We love your kids from afar, and you deserve a night out!


Can I Bring a Guest or +1?

To keep the celebration intimate, we are keeping the guest list limited. Please do not bring anyone who was not invited.



A Note on Vaping/Smoking

Please do not smoke or vape in the venue, including the outdoor balcony. Anyone doing so will be politely asked to leave.
