The Story

Melissa Tran & Adam Araza

  • December 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Melissa and Adam met on a dating app called Coffee Meets Bagel. Adam gave the impression he knew how to scuba dive and Melissa slyly indicated she did not live with her parents.

  • February 2, 2016

    A Relationship is Born

    Before a spontaneous trip to San Diego, their relationship became official! Adam officially asked Melissa out at a mediocre boba shop.

  • April 15, 2017

    The Start of A Life in Sin

    Much to the dismay of their Asian parents, Adam, Melissa, and Waffles decided to take their relationship to next level by shacking up together at an apartment in Sunnyvale, CA.

  • January 27, 2020

    A Promise to Cleanse the Sin

    Weighed down by the guilt of living together while not being married, Melissa and Adam agreed to fight their sinful demons by agreeing to a marriage pact.
